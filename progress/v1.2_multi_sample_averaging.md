# v1.2 — Multi-Sample Averaging (Breakthrough)

**Date:** 2026-02-27
**Goal:** Test multi-sample averaging — take N noisy readings of the same ground-truth state and average them.

## Method
- Access ground truth from PhysX simulation state
- Generate N synthetic noisy readings by adding Gaussian noise to ground truth
- Average the N samples → noise reduces by factor 1/sqrt(N)
- Feed averaged observation to policy actor

## Key Insight
Unlike EMA filtering (which smooths across TIME and introduces lag),
multi-sample averaging smooths across SAMPLES at the SAME timestep.
No lag, just noise reduction.

## Results (N=5 samples)

| Noise Level | Vanilla Success | Multi-Sample Success | Delta    |
|-------------|----------------|---------------------|----------|
| Medium      | 92.5%          | **100.0%**          | **+7.5%**  |
| High        | 58.0%          | **96.1%**           | **+38.1%** |
